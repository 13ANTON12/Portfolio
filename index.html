About Me

I design robust analog and mixed-signal circuits that translate mathematical rigor into manufacturable silicon. My approach blends simulation discipline with layout awareness, ensuring architectures meet noise, linearity, and power targets while remaining process-tolerant.

I focus on closing the loop between theory and tapeout: modeling, corner analysis, and verification inform topology choices and device sizing, while parasitic-aware layout and extraction validate performance across PVT. I value concise documentation and reproducible flows.
